Cocogoat Cafe

Cocogoat Cafe: a Cozy Comfort Zone

Cocogoat Cafe is a pop-up cafe based on the game Genshin Impact. (see video) We serve dishes and drinks that can be found in game! We have multiple branches across Teyvat, and more to come in every region! Affordable prices, beautiful presentation, and great, tasty food, served for you by our cooks. Our cute little cafe, run by our resident Qilin, Ganyu, is the best cafe* in all of Teyvat. With a simple concept of uniting cuisine from all regions, having a wide array in our menu promotes diversity and discovering new cuisines and ingredients.

*note: we are the only cafe in Teyvat, thus making us best by default. this is not indicative of quality.

Come check out the menu! We've got food and drink from all over Teyvat, be it Mondstadt or Sumeru. With 8 dishes and 5 drinks, you're sure to find at least something you'd enjoy. Our menu will keep expanding and include new meals in the future the more we explore other cultures and cuisines. Be sure to check the menu page of our website to have an in-depth look at our menu before you head out to one of our locations. See you there!
